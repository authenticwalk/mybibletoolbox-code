### Recommended Tiers

**TIER 1: Full Tool 2 Treatment** (~50 rare words)

**TIER 2: Consider Tool 2** (~100 rare words)
- Words appearing 2-5x with theological significance

**TIER 3: Tool 1 Only** (Most rare words)
- Descriptive terms (body parts, objects, actions)
- No significant cultural/theological complexity
- Adequately explained by lexicons alone

### Process Adjustments for Rare Words

1. **Lower Source Expectations:** 5-10 sources vs. 10-15 for common words
2. **Leverage Classical Sources:** Perseus, MIT Classics Archive essential
3. **Prioritize Diachronic Analysis:** Classical → Koine → Patristic → Medieval
4. **Focus on Value-Add:** Ask "What does Tool 2 provide beyond lexicons?"
5. **Time Management:** 90-120 minutes (vs. 150-180 for common words)
