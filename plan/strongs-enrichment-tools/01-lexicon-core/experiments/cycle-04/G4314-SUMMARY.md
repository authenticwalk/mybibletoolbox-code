## Key Features

### Case Functions

**Accusative (95% of usage):**
1. Directional - motion toward goal
2. Communicative - speech/address to recipient
3. Purposive - intended end (πρὸς τό + infinitive)
4. Relational - concerning, with regard to
5. Temporal - duration/approximation
6. Adversative - hostile direction (context-dependent)
7. Proximity - static location near

**Genitive (<1%, 6x NT):**

### Theological Significance

**Christological:**
- John 1:1 - ὁ λόγος ἦν πρὸς τὸν θεόν (Word was with God)
- Expresses eternal face-to-face communion between Father and Son

**Soteriological:**
- Coming to Christ (ἔρχομαι πρός)
- Faith toward God (πίστις πρὸς τὸν θεόν)
- Access to Father (προσαγωγὴ πρὸς τὸν πατέρα - Eph 2:18)
- Peace with God (εἰρήνη πρὸς τὸν θεόν - Rom 5:1)

**Ecclesiological:**
- πρὸς ἀλλήλους (toward one another) - mutual love, service
- Conduct toward outsiders (πρὸς τοὺς ἔξω)

**Eschatological:**
- πρόσωπον πρὸς πρόσωπον (face to face - 1 Cor 13:12)
- Sufferings compared to glory (Rom 8:18)
