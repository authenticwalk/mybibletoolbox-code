# Production Rollout: 20-Word Validation Batch

**Date:** 2025-11-11
**Status:** Planning
**Purpose:** Validate Cycle 4 production methodology across diverse word types

---

## Overview

This is the first production rollout after Cycle 4 completion. We'll extract 20 carefully selected words representing the full diversity of the 14,000 Strong's corpus to validate:

1. Consistency of Cycle 4 methodology
2. Time and richness targets across word types
3. Quality assurance processes
4. Edge case handling

---

## Word Selection Criteria

**Diversity Requirements:**
- 8 complex grammatical words (40%)
- 4 particles (20%)
- 8 theological words (40%)
- Mix of Greek and Hebrew
- Range of frequency tiers (hapax → ultra-high)
- Include some challenging/controversial words

---

## Selected Words

### Category 1: Complex Grammatical Words (8 words)

#### Pronouns (3 words)
1. **G3778 οὗτος** (this) - 1,387 occurrences
   - Demonstrative pronoun
   - Complex morphology (24+ forms)
   - Near/far distinction with ἐκεῖνος
   - **Pathway:** Cycle 4 Grammatical
   - **Expected:** 47 min, 8.3/10

2. **G1473 ἐγώ** (I) - 1,718 occurrences
   - Personal pronoun (first person)
   - 8 forms (nominative to dative)
   - Emphatic vs. standard usage
   - **Pathway:** Cycle 4 Grammatical
   - **Expected:** 47 min, 8.3/10

3. **G5100 τις** (someone, anyone) - 543 occurrences
   - Indefinite pronoun
   - Interrogative vs. indefinite functions
   - Morphological complexity
   - **Pathway:** Cycle 4 Grammatical
   - **Expected:** 48 min, 8.2/10

#### Prepositions (3 words)
4. **G1722 ἐν** (in) - 2,743 occurrences
   - Ultra-high frequency preposition
   - 8+ functional categories (location, instrument, sphere)
   - Always with dative
   - **Pathway:** Cycle 4 Grammatical
   - **Expected:** 47 min, 8.3/10

5. **G1537 ἐκ** (from, out of) - 916 occurrences
   - High frequency preposition
   - Source, origin, partitive functions
   - Always with genitive
   - **Pathway:** Cycle 4 Grammatical
   - **Expected:** 47 min, 8.3/10

6. **G4314 πρός** (to, toward) - 700 occurrences
   - Directional preposition
   - Multiple case functions (accusative primary, genitive, dative)
   - Rich semantic range
   - **Pathway:** Cycle 4 Grammatical
   - **Expected:** 48 min, 8.3/10

#### Articles/Conjunctions (2 words)
7. **G3588 ὁ/ἡ/τό** (the) - 19,870 occurrences
   - Most frequent NT word
   - Definite article with 24 forms
   - Substantivizer function
   - **Pathway:** Cycle 4 Grammatical (may need special handling)
   - **Expected:** 50 min, 8.0/10 (high freq may dilute richness)

8. **G2532 καί** (and, also, even) - 9,153 occurrences
   - Most frequent conjunction
   - 3+ functional categories (coordinating, adverbial, ascensive)
   - Actually a particle, but testing as grammatical first
   - **Pathway:** Cycle 4 Particle OR Grammatical
   - **Expected:** 48 min, 8.7/10 (if particle pathway)

## Workflow Plan

### Phase 1: Setup (0.5 hrs)
1. Create production folder structure
2. Set up tracking spreadsheet (word, type, time, richness, validation)
3. Prepare templates (grammatical, particle, theological, Hebrew)
4. Review Cycle 4 methodology documents

### Phase 2: Extraction (17.5-18 hrs across multiple days)

**Day 1-2: Grammatical Words (8 words, ~6.5 hrs)**
- Words 1-8: Pronouns, prepositions, articles
- Apply Cycle 4 Grammatical pathway
- Track time per component (morphology, diachronic, syntax, etc.)

**Day 3: Particles (4 words, ~3.2 hrs)**
- Words 9-12: γάρ, οὖν, ἀλλά, μέν
- Apply Cycle 4 Particle pathway
- Validate discourse grammar integration

**Day 4-5: Greek Theological (6 words, ~6 hrs)**
- Words 13-18: ἀγάπη, πίστις, ἁμαρτία, χάρις, λόγος, καρδία
- Apply Cycle 3 Theological pathway
- Full controversy detection

**Day 6: Hebrew Words (2 words, ~2.1 hrs)**
- Words 19-20: YHWH, hesed
- Apply Cycle 3 Hebrew pathway
- BDB/TWOT primary sources

### Phase 3: Validation (4 hrs)
1. Run Level 1 validation on all 20 words (2 hrs)
2. Run Level 2 validation on all 20 words (1.5 hrs)
3. Calculate richness scores (0.5 hrs)

### Phase 4: Analysis (2 hrs)
1. Time analysis (actual vs. targets)
2. Richness analysis (distribution, outliers)
3. Edge case documentation
4. Methodology adjustments (if needed)

### Phase 5: Documentation (1 hr)
1. Create 20-word batch summary
2. Update CURRENT-STATE.md
3. Commit and push results

**Total Estimated Time:** 25 hours (over 6-10 days)

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Time Overruns
**Mitigation:**
- Start with 2 words/day pace (low pressure)
- If consistently faster, accelerate to 4 words/day
- Build in buffer days

### Risk 2: Quality Issues
**Mitigation:**
- Run validation after every 4 words (not just at end)
- Address issues immediately before continuing
- Don't rush to meet timeline if quality suffers

### Risk 3: Edge Cases
**Mitigation:**
- Document all unexpected challenges
- Create fallback strategies for difficult words
- Note any words that need special handling

### Risk 4: Context Overload
**Mitigation:**
- Use subagents for parallel extraction
- Keep main context focused on orchestration
- Archive completed words immediately
