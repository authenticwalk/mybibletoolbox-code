## Rule 2.7 Summary

**Outsider Quoting In-Group → EXCLUSIVE**

```
IF outer speaker = outsider/hostile party
AND outer speaker quotes in-group speech (hypothetically or actually)
AND outer speaker is explicitly excluded from in-group
THEN clusivity = EXCLUSIVE (in-group excludes quoter)
```

**Confidence**: 75% (medium)

## Key Observations

### Rule 2.7 Application (5 verses)

Rule 2.7 was specifically designed for quoted speech scenarios. Applied to:
- Isaiah 36:7 (Assyrian quoting Jerusalem) - THE example case
- Ezekiel 20:27-29 (God quoting ancestors)
- Ezekiel 36:20-23 (God quoting nations about Israel)
- Revelation 3:17 (Christ quoting church)
- Possibly Malachi 3:8 (God quoting people)

### Dominant Pattern: Group Distinction (Rule 3.2)

8 verses show ethnic, religious, or social group boundaries:
- Enemies vs Jerusalem (Lam 2:15-16)
- Rebels vs Davidic dynasty (1 Kings 12:16)
- Witnesses vs Jesus (Matt 26, Mark 14)
- Jews vs nations (Esther, Ezekiel)

### We/You Contrast (Rule 2.3)

7 verses show explicit or implicit speaker/addressee distinction:
- People vs leaders (Numbers 20, 1 Samuel 8)
- Elders vs Jephthah (Judges 11)
- We...you patterns in quoted speech

### Low Confidence Cases (2 verses)

- Song of Solomon 1:4 (70%) - Unclear speaker boundaries
- Song of Solomon 8:12 (65%) - Possible lack of first-person plural

These reflect genuine ambiguity in Song's poetic structure.

## Validation Notes

### Critical for Algorithm v2.1

Rule 2.7 was added in v2.1 specifically to handle quoted speech (fixing 2 Kings 18:22 error). This category provides the key test for:

1. **Rule 2.7 effectiveness**: Does it correctly identify outsider quoting patterns?
2. **Nested analysis**: Can the algorithm handle multiple levels of quotation?
3. **Rule interaction**: How does 2.7 interact with 2.1, 2.3, and 3.2 in complex cases?

### Expected Challenges

1. **Unclear quotation boundaries** (Song of Solomon verses)
2. **Multiple nested levels** (Jeremiah 42:20, Ezekiel passages)
3. **Divine vs human outsider** (Does God count as "outsider" for Rule 2.7?)
4. **Hypothetical vs actual quotes** (Hosea 6:1-2)
