## Validation Rules

### Auto-Approved Content
Content is auto-approved when:
- All sources have Authority: High
- No LLM sources present
- No cultural adaptations noted
- No theological debates present
- No textual variants with significant impact

### Requires Human Review
Content requires human review when:
- ANY source has `requires_validation: true`
- ANY source has Authority: Low
- Cultural adaptation noted
- Theological debate present
- Textual variant with significant impact
- Mixed authority levels (High + Low/Medium)
